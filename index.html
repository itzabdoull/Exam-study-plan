🧠
Spaced Repetition
Review topics 1 day, 3 days, and 7 days before each exam. Short frequent reviews beat cramming.
⏱️
Pomodoro Sessions
Study 45 mins, break 15 mins. Never study more than 3 hours in one subject without switching.
📝
Practice Past Questions
Spend 40% of study time solving past exam questions. Theory alone won't cut it.
💤
Sleep Before Exams
Sleep consolidates memory. Avoid all-nighters the day before. Be in bed by 11 PM the night before each exam.
🗓️
Double Exam Days
Apr 13 has TWO exams (CMP 411B + 306B). Start preparing both from Apr 6.
